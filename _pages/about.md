Welcome to my website! I'm a Predoctoral Research Fellow at [Microsoft Research's Economics & Computation Group](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/theme/economics-and-computation/) currently working on projects at the intersection of labor economics, behavioral economics, and game theory. Before that, I worked for a year as a Data Analyst at One Drop and graduated from UC Berkeley in 2021 with a double major in Economics and Computer Science. 

I am highly interested in studying both the drivers of technology and the impacts of modern technology on human decision-making. Current and past research that I have been involved with includes projects related to how AI has affected jobs in the healthcare industry, the spread of misinformation in social networks, and understanding how users respond to streaming-related ads on search engines. Outside of work I enjoy playing soccer and basketball, philosophy, listening to music, and stand up comedy. 

You can reach me at **sidsatya.research@gmail.com**. Thanks for visiting!
